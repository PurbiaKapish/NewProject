📋 Terms & Conditions

1. About Us

Iconic Prints is a Polaroid photo printing service operating through the Meesho platform. By using this website and uploading your photos, you agree to the following terms.

2. Photo Submission

  • You confirm that you own or have the right to use all photos you submit.
  • You are responsible for the content of the photos you upload.
  • We will not print photos that are illegal, offensive, or violate any third-party rights.
  • Photos are uploaded solely for the purpose of fulfilling your print order.

3. Order Confirmation

  • Your order is confirmed only after you send the WhatsApp confirmation message.
  • Uploading photos does not guarantee an order without WhatsApp confirmation.
  • We will begin processing your order once the WhatsApp message is received.

4. Print Quality

  • We recommend uploading original, high-resolution photos for the best print quality.
  • Low-resolution photos may result in blurry prints. We will warn you about this during upload.
  • We are not responsible for quality issues caused by low-resolution photos submitted by the customer.

5. Cancellations & Returns

  • Orders can be cancelled before printing begins. Contact us immediately on WhatsApp if you wish to cancel.
  • Once printing has started, cancellations may not be possible.
  • Returns or replacements are considered in case of a printing error on our part.

6. Delivery

Delivery is handled through Meesho's logistics. Delivery times depend on your location and Meesho's shipping schedule. We are not responsible for delivery delays caused by Meesho or logistics partners.

7. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of our service after changes means you accept the updated terms.

🔒 Privacy Policy

What information we collect

  • Phone number — your WhatsApp number for order communication
  • Meesho Order ID — to match your print order
  • Photos — the images you upload for printing
  • Order details — pack size, quantity, and timestamp

How we use your information

  • Your photos and order details are saved to our private Google Drive to fulfil your print order.
  • Your phone number is used only to contact you about your order via WhatsApp.
  • We do not sell, share, or use your information for advertising or any purpose other than fulfilling your order.

Photo storage & deletion

  • Your photos are stored securely in our private Google Drive.
  • Photos are kept for up to 30 days after your order is delivered, then deleted.
  • You can request deletion of your photos at any time by contacting us on WhatsApp.

Security

Your photos and personal data are stored in a private, password-protected Google Drive accessible only to us. We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Third-party services

  • Google Drive — used to store your order photos and details
  • WhatsApp — used for order confirmation and communication
  • Tesseract.js — used for OCR (Order ID detection from screenshots). This runs entirely in your browser — your screenshots are never sent to any server for OCR processing
  • Meesho — used for selling and delivery

Your rights

  • You can request to see what data we have about you.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Contact us on WhatsApp for any privacy-related requests.

Contact for privacy concerns

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us directly on WhatsApp. See the Contact Us page for details.
