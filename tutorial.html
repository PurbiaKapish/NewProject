📋 Step-by-step Guide

1

Enter your WhatsApp number

Type your 10-digit WhatsApp mobile number (without +91). We use this to contact you if needed.

2

Enter your Meesho Order ID

You can type your Order ID, or take a screenshot of your Meesho order page and upload it — our system will automatically read the Order ID for you!

🔍 How to find your Order ID on Meesho

  1. Open the Meesho app
  2. Tap "My Orders" at the bottom
  3. Select your order
  4. The Order ID is shown at the top of the page (e.g. 40512345678)
  5. Take a screenshot and upload it, or type it here
3

Choose your Pack & Quantity

Select how many Polaroid photos you want (9, 16, 18, 25, 36, 48, 50, or 54) and the quantity you ordered on Meesho.

4

Upload your photos

Tap the upload area and select your photos. Important: upload original photos, not screenshots. We need original high-resolution photos for the best print quality.

⚠️ If a photo shows a "Low Resolution" warning, the print quality may not be perfect. We recommend replacing it with a higher-quality photo.
5

Preview (Optional)

Turn on the Polaroid Frame Preview to see how each photo will look when printed. Drag the image inside the frame to reposition it. This step is optional — you can skip it.

6

Review & Submit

Check all your details on the Order Summary page, then tap "Submit Photos."

7

Confirm on WhatsApp

After submitting, a WhatsApp message will be ready for you. Tap "Open WhatsApp" and send it to confirm your order. Your photos will be saved and we'll start printing!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are Polaroid photos?

Polaroids are small square photo prints with a white border, just like classic instant camera photos. They look beautiful as gifts, decorations, or memories!

How long does delivery take?

Delivery is handled through Meesho. Your order will be processed after you confirm on WhatsApp, and shipping will happen as per Meesho's timeline.

What photo format is best?

JPG, PNG, or HEIC formats all work. For the best print quality, make sure photos are at least 800×800 pixels. Original camera photos are always best.

What if my Order ID is wrong?

Don't worry — you can go back and correct it. Make sure the Order ID matches exactly what's shown in the Meesho app.

What if I face any issue?

Contact us directly on WhatsApp! We're here to help. Visit the Contact Us page.
